Description of Available Services
  • Conduct performance tests for fire and life safety systems.
  • Ensure compliance with emergency planning and evacuation requirements.
  • Investigate fire-related incidents.
  • Conduct fire/life safety audits.
  • Conduct fire prevention/life safety training.
  • Research and interpret building and fire code issues.
  • Review life safety and ADA code compliance of existing facilities and new construction/renovation projects. (The University Fire Marshal is the authority having jurisdiction for review and approval of construction/renovation projects involving less than $100,000. To obtain this service see Construction Plan Review/Approval Policy.)

Procedure to Obtain Services

Services can be obtained by contacting the University Fire Marshal's office by E-mail or by phone.
