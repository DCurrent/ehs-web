RECYCLE CONTAINER
LOCATION POLICY

The following has been established as the location policy for recycling containers. For additional information on the type of container to use, contact the Recycling Coordinator at 257-8788.

  • Containers shall not be located in exit stairwells,
  • Containers shall not be located in an exit corridor less than 6' in width,
  • Containers shall not be located in a manner to cause an unsafe condition for a physically impaired person,
  • Containers shall not be located beneath bulletin boards,
  • Containers shall not be located side by side except in individual rooms,
  • Containers used shall be demonstrated to be flame resistant (non-supporting self-combustion) and approved by the University Fire Marshal, and
  • When located in an area other than an individual room, the container shall be in a "public oriented" area.

Questions concerning this Policy may be addressed to the University Fire Marshal at (859) 257-6326.
