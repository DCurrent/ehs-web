Procedure to Obtain Service
  • General: Certain members of the University community have need for specific, technical environmental services or specialized laboratory analyses. These services are provided by Environmental Management or one of the University's environmental consultants. In most cases, services provided by Environmental Management are free of charge to the units.
  • Contact Environmental Management at the phone number listed above to request these services. Requests should include as much specific project information as possible, such as:
    • specific service being requested
    • location or project area
    • site contact
    • special arragements for access, if needed
    • reason for the request
    • timeframe or schedule needed
    • account number to be charged
